Favorite places

Langano

One of the places we visited most growing up was Langano. It was a family favorite, and thus we frequented it the most. Its beaches and the warm lake were comforting, and some of my favorite memories happened right at the shore.

Hawassa

Hawassa and langano went hand in hand, when we were kids we did not visit one without visiting the other.
When we went there as kids, Hawassa was so green and brimming with nature, every short walk doesn't seem short at all. The animals seemed to be our friends. The water making it nice and cold. Hawassa was certainly a favorite

Wendo Genet

Wendo genet was hands down my favorite paradise. On top of the fact that it's surname is my mother's name, wendo genet had so much to offer to young me. Though it has been years since i've vistied, and we didn't visit nearly as much as we did all our other destinations, it still had the greatest effect on me and is -to this day- my most favorite place
